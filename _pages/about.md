## Education & Research
Bachelor of Engineering @[School of Computer Science](http://cs.ccnu.edu.cn/English.htm), [Central China Normal University](http://english.ccnu.edu.cn/). <br/>
Master student @[Wuhan Botanical Garden](http://english.wbg.cas.cn/), [CAS](http://english.cas.cn/), under the supervision of Prof. [Wang Hengchang](http://sourcedb.wbg.cas.cn/yw/rckyw/200907/t20090720_2708418.html). My research interest mainly lies in Plant Taxonomy, Systematics, Evolution and Phytogeography. And the focal area is Qinghai-Tibet Plateau. <br/>
Favourite Plants: *Androsace umbellata*, and all other *Androsace* species. <br/>
Other Groups of Interest: Primulaceae, Araceae, Fagaceae, Papaveraceae, and Commelinaceae.<br/>
